Waterford Greenway

Take in stunning seaside views, rolling countryside, castles, viaducts and more.
A dedicated cycling and walking route following the old railway line between Dungarvan and Waterford City. This route is paved the whole way and is free from cars and vehicles. The route passes through towns and villages with plenty of shops and cafe's to stop at.

FAQ's

Can anyone join?

This club is open to adults of all skill levels.

Is there a fee?

Nope, just turn up to any events you want to do.

What do I need?

A bike, helmet, comfy clothes, plenty of water, light snacks.

Will any bike do?

Yes any bike will do once you are comfortable cycling it for more than 30 minutes. We would recommend a bike with gears for tackling any hills.
