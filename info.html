About Us

Beginnings

This club was set up in mid 2017 by a group of friends
who just wanted to go out for a leisurely cycle.

We started off small with the odd 20km loop on a sunny day and
built it up from there. Our longest cycle to date has been 100km.

What does it involve?

A typical cycle is almost always on a pleasent dry or sunny day.
Routes with a pub or cafe along the way are highly prioritised.
A normal halfway stop involves a beer or two accompanied by a
pub lunch or fresh pizza depending on the route.

Who can join?

Anyone, we pride ourselves on being laid back and casual compared
to other cycling clubs. You won't find skintight latex and counting
milliseconds off personal records here. We just like the exercise,
fresh air, nice scenery and socialising.
